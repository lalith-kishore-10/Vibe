Vibe.io Community Guidelines

Building a supportive space for Tamil Nadu's student developers.

Our Philosophy

Welcome to Vibe.io! Our mission is to create the most supportive, collaborative, and innovative community for student developers in Tamil Nadu. This is a place to learn, share, and grow together. To ensure our community remains a safe and productive space for everyone, we ask that you read and follow these guidelines. Our goal isn't to restrict you, but to empower everyone to have the best possible experience.

Section 1: The Core Rules - Keep Our Community Healthy

These rules are essential for maintaining a respectful and safe environment. Violating these rules will result in moderation action.

Be Respectful and Collaborative.

  • Treat every member with respect. Harassment, bullying, personal insults, or any form of intimidation will not be tolerated.
  • Healthy debate is welcome, but personal attacks are not. Criticize ideas, not people.
  • Remember that we have members from various backgrounds and skill levels. Be patient with beginners and humble as an expert.

No Hate Speech or Discrimination.

  • Vibe.io has a zero-tolerance policy for any content that attacks or demeans an individual or group based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other personal attribute.

Keep it Safe for Everyone (Zero Tolerance).

  • Do not share or post any content that is graphically violent, sexually explicit, or promotes self-harm.
  • Do not make threats of violence or harm against anyone. Any credible threat will result in an immediate ban and may be reported to the relevant authorities.

Respect Privacy.

  • Do not share anyone's private, personal information without their explicit consent (this is often called "doxxing"). This includes addresses, phone numbers, private photos, or any other identifying data.

Section 2: The Platform Rules - Keep Our Community Focused

These rules help ensure that conversations are productive, on-topic, and valuable for everyone.

Stay On-Topic.

  • Please post your questions and discussions in the appropriate channels or forums (e.g., post Python questions in the #python channel). This keeps the community organized and makes it easier for others to find information.

No Spam or Excessive Self-Promotion.

  • Spamming includes posting the same message repeatedly, sending unsolicited DMs, or posting irrelevant links.
  • It's okay to share your projects and achievements! However, please do so in the designated #project-showcase channels. Do not excessively promote your personal social media, paid products, or services in general discussion channels.

Share Knowledge Responsibly.

  • Do not encourage or facilitate cheating or academic dishonesty. It's okay to help someone with a concept or debug a piece of code, but do not provide complete solutions to graded college assignments. The goal is to learn, not to cheat.
  • Do not share harmful misinformation. Do not post content that is demonstrably false and could cause harm (e.g., promoting malware, phishing scams, or dangerous coding practices).

Respect Intellectual Property.

  • Only share content that you have the right to share. Do not post links to pirated software, books, or courses. Give credit where it's due when sharing code or resources from others.

Section 3: How We Enforce These Rules

Our moderation team, which includes our Student Ambassadors, is here to ensure these guidelines are followed. If you see someone breaking the rules, please report it.

Reporting

  • Use the "Report" feature on the platform or contact a moderator or ambassador directly. Provide as much detail as possible.

Our Process

  • Warning: For minor infractions, you will receive a friendly warning from a moderator.
  • Temporary Mute/Suspension: For repeated minor offenses or more serious violations, your account may be temporarily muted or suspended.
  • Permanent Ban: For severe violations like hate speech, threats, or spamming, your account will be permanently removed from Vibe.io.

We believe in being fair and transparent. Our primary goal is to educate and maintain a healthy community, not to punish.

Thank you for being a part of Vibe.io and helping us build a great community. Let's code, connect, and conquer together!
